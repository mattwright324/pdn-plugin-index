Paint.NET Plugin Index PDNPI
    Revision Date: 1 June 2018
This application simplifies locating Paint.NET plugins by searching the Plugin Index. The app was originally built by Matthew Wright (SodiumEnglish) and is maintained by he and Scott Stringer (Ego Eram Reputo). Updates are published monthly, usually around the first of the month. Usage: use the sidebar to enter search terms and filter the results.
